Last Updated: February 2026

1. Data Collection

At RedCast, we prioritize your privacy. We do not store any personal data or download history. The URLs you submit are processed solely for the purpose of provideing the download stream and are not logged or saved.

We use cookies and local storage technologies strictly to improve your experience (e.g., remembering your preferred quality settings).

We may display non-intrusive advertisements from third-party networks. These services may collect anonymous data according to their own privacy policies to serve relevant ads.

4. Security

All communications between your browser and our servers are encrypted using SSL (HTTPS).
